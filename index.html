Jacq François

Artist's bio

Jacq François (b. 1990) is a NOLA-based visual artist whose illustrations emphasize the importance of femininity & crisis. Solely using ballpoint pens, Jacq's main goal is to bring awareness to sociological dilemmas & juxtapose the ideas of soft beauty with harsh issues.
